About

Tortas are the mexican version of a sandwich. Different ingredients can go inside, depending on the type of torta that you are having.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Bread
  • Mayo
  • Avocado
  • Cheese
  • Beans
  • Salsa

How to prepare:

  1. Get the bread ready, cut in half
  2. Spread some mayo inside
  3. Put the meat inside
  4. Thrown in some cheese, avocado and beans
  5. Add some beans if you want
  6. Heat it up in a pan, first on one side, then the other one
  7. Add as much salsa as you like
  8. Enjoy!
