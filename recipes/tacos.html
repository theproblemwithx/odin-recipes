Tacos al Pastor

About

Tacos al pastor are famous all over Mexico. The basic recipe is pork meat with a special seasoning, cilantro, onion, and a slice of pineapple.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Pineapple
  • Salsa

How to prepare:

  1. Prepare the pork
  2. Heat the tortillas
  3. Slice it off the trompo
  4. Put it inside the tortillas
  5. Throw some onion and cilantro
  6. Top it with the pineapple
  7. Add as much salsa as you like
  8. Enjoy!
