About

Tamales are a food that goes back to the pre-columbian times. It involves dough wrapped in a banana leaf, which in turn is filled with different meats such as pork, chicken or beef.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Banan Leaf
  • Dough
  • Fat
  • Salsa

How to prepare:

  1. Prepare the meat
  2. Prepare the dough
  3. Add the meat to the dough
  4. Put inside of the banana leaves
  5. Cook inside of pot
  6. Unwrap and enjoy
